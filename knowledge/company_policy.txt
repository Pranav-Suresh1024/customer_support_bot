# Company Policy

## Refunds and Cancellations
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of their initial purchase for any reason. 
After 30 days, refunds are only issued on a pro-rated basis for annual subscriptions, and only if the service has experienced significant downtime (more than 48 hours in a given month) that was our fault.
All refund requests must be routed to the Billing department and must be approved by management. 
Subscription cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. To cancel, a user must request it, which triggers a management review process.

## Escalations
Any request for compensation, or explicit requests to speak with a manager or escalate an issue, must be flagged for management approval.

## Account Policies
Accounts that are inactive for 12 months will be suspended.
To reactivate, users must contact the Account department.
Account closure requests require management approval.
