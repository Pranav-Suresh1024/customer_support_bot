# Pricing Guide

## Plans
We offer three main pricing plans:

1. Basic Plan: $10/month or $100/year. Includes 1 user, 10GB storage, and standard support (email only).
2. Professional Plan: $25/month or $250/year. Includes 5 users, 100GB storage, and priority support (email and chat).
3. Enterprise Plan: $100/month or $1000/year. Includes unlimited users, 1TB storage, 24/7 phone support, and a dedicated account manager.

## Add-ons
- Extra Storage: $5/month per additional 50GB.
- Extra User (Basic/Pro): $5/month per user.

All subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled before the billing date.
