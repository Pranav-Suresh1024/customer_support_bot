# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I change my billing cycle from monthly to annual?
A: You can change your billing cycle in the Account Settings -> Billing tab. The change will take effect immediately and you will be charged a prorated amount.

Q: Do you offer a free trial?
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for the Professional Plan. No credit card is required.

Q: Can I merge two accounts?
A: Account merging is currently not supported. You must choose one account to keep and manually transfer data, then request closure of the other account.

Q: How fast is standard support?
A: Standard support queries are typically answered within 24 hours on business days.
